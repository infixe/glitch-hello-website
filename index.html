About GrabBuddy

What

A money management functionality embedded within the Grab app.

Why

Technology has changed the way we bank, make purchases and track them, and today we use are phones in place of cash. Kids learn by doing and they need to know how things work. It's important for kids to learn how to use banking products from a young age and to learn about their advantages and how to use them safely.

Who

For parents
  • Schedule and automate allowance
  • Review kids’ spending habits and set up spending controls with real-time notifications on anomalies
  • Help kids set money milestones as they work towards their savings goals
  • Assign quests so children can learn about earning money
For children
  • Mobile wallet to track allowance and available balance
  • Interactive dashboard to track spending habits
  • Wishlists and savings goals
  • A safer way to spend with virtual debit cards and parental controls
  • Friendly competition between peers
